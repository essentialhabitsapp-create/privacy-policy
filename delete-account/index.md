# Delete Your Essential Habits Account & Data

**Last updated: June 2026**

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the Essential Habits app. This action cannot be undone.

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the **Essential Habits** app.
2. Go to **Account** (profile icon in the top-right).
3. Tap **Delete my account**.
4. Read the confirmation message carefully.
5. Tap **Delete permanently** to confirm.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your account credentials (email and authentication records)
- All habits, tasks, goals, and notes stored in the cloud
- Your habit history and progress data
- All sync and backup data

## What Is NOT Deleted

Data stored **locally on your device** in guest mode is not affected by account deletion. If you were using guest mode alongside an account, you can clear local data by:

- Going to **Settings → Reset app data**, or
- Uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

## Processing Time

Account deletion is processed immediately. Cloud-stored data is permanently removed from our servers within 24 hours. Backup copies may persist for up to 30 days in secure storage before being fully purged.
