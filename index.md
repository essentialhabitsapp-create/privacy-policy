# Privacy Policy for Essential Habits

**Last updated: June 2025**

## About the App

Essential Habits is a personal productivity app that helps you track daily habits,
tasks, long-term goals, and notes. It is available on the Google Play Store.

## Data We Collect

Essential Habits collects only the information you voluntarily provide:

- **Email address** — used solely for account creation and authentication.
- **App content** — the habits, tasks, goals, and notes you create within the app.
- **App preferences** — your theme choice (light/dark) and notification settings.

We do **not** collect: precise location data, device identifiers, contacts, photos,
health data, browsing history, or any information from other apps on your device.

## How We Use Your Data

- To authenticate your account and sync your data across devices you own.
- To display your habits, tasks, goals, and notes within the app.
- To send habit reminder notifications (only if you enable them).

Your data is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality. It is never
used for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored:

1. **Locally** on your device using secure local storage.
2. **In the cloud** on Supabase (our backend provider) so you can sync across
   devices and recover your data if you change phones.

Supabase servers are hosted in secure data centers with industry-standard encryption.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

- **Supabase** — provides authentication and cloud database storage. Supabase
  processes your email and app content solely for the purpose of operating the
  backend. Supabase's privacy policy is available at:
  [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

We do not use any analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporters, or
tracking tools.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. Your
data is only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data at any time — everything you've entered is visible within the app.
- **Delete** your data — you can delete individual items (habits, tasks, goals, notes)
  or reset all app data from the Settings screen.
- **Delete your account** — go to the settings screen and tap "Delete my account".
  This permanently removes your cloud-stored data. Local data can be cleared by
  uninstalling the app.
- **Use the app without an account** — a guest mode is available that stores all
  data locally on your device without any cloud storage.

For any data-related requests, contact us at the email below.

## Data Retention

- **With an account**: Data is stored in the cloud until you delete it or delete
  your account.
- **Guest mode**: Data is stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app
  permanently removes all local data.
- **Account deletion**: When you sign out and choose to delete your data, cloud
  records are removed within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Essential Habits is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe
your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will
delete it promptly.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on
this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this
policy periodically.
